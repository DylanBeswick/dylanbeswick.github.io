My First Time Ziplining

A Poem Written by Dylan Beswick

All I see is black
Am I blind?
All I can do is walk.
Walk.
Forward.
For.
All.
Eternity.
It’s somehow darker than pitch black.
After forever, a wall hits me.
A wall with a door…
A wall with a door with a doorknob…
A wall with a door with a doorknob with an empty keyhole…
A wall with a door with a doorknob with a key strung around it…
I take the dusty key and unlock the door with it.
I look through and see:


And make the mistake of entering the room.
I fall into infinite darkness until I see:

So I put away my video game and go outside.
